The game should work with any list of questions and their answers. For example, based on the Hangman game, DeepSeek devised a game that I transformed into one based on Mark 4:28. - "Grain that yields a stalk and grain" based on Mark 4:28 (positive Hangman)

* It starts with a single grain planted in dirt
* When the percentage of correct answers > 25%, the stalk is drawn.
* When the percentage of correct answers > 50%, the leaf is drawn on the stalk.
* When the percentage of correct answers > 75%, small grains are drawn on the stalk with leaves.
* When the percentage of correct answers is 100%, the stalk with leaves and mature grains is drawn.
