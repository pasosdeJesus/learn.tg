# R-#158: Yield Strategy for Idle Cluster/Country Funds (pdJ Custody)

Funds in `ClusterFunds` sit idle while waiting for GD contact + release (weeks to months). Since pdJ custodies these funds, pdJ can put them to work safely.

## Distinction from #159

| REQ | Who holds funds | Who decides yield | When |
|-----|----------------|-------------------|------|
| #158 | pdJ (ClusterFunds contract) | pdJ | Before release |
| #159 | Cluster (multisig wallet) | Cluster | After release |

## Yield Distribution

| Recipient | % | Purpose |
|-----------|-----|---------|
| Cluster/Country Fund | 70% | Added to the same fund, visible in ranking |
| pdJ Treasury | 30% | Covers operational costs (servers, APIs, development) |

**Design principle:** Most yield stays with the cluster that generated it. pdJ takes a smaller share to cover real operational costs — transparent and visible in the dashboard.

## Policy

- **Only low-risk, liquid protocols** (AAVE V3 for USDT, stCELO for CELO)
- **Yield accrues to the same cluster/country fund** — 70% stays with the fund, 30% to pdJ Treasury
- **Transparent:** yield and distribution visible in ranking and cluster page alongside principal
- **Admin-only:** deposits and withdrawals via pdJ backend (signed with `PRIVATE_KEY`)
- **Auto-withdrawal on release:** when `releaseClusterFunds()` is called, principal + yield (net of pdJ share) is withdrawn and sent to cluster multisig

## Phase 1: AAVE V3 (USDT)

| Protocol | Asset | Risk |
|----------|-------|------|
| AAVE V3 on Celo | USDT | Low — well-audited, liquid |

Implementation: a helper contract or backend script that pdJ can call to deposit idle USDT from `ClusterFunds` into AAVE. The aUSDT is held by the contract. On `releaseClusterFunds()`, the contract withdraws from AAVE first, distributes 30% yield to pdJ Treasury and 70% to the cluster, then transfers USDT (principal + 70% yield) to the cluster multisig.

## Phase 2: stCELO (CELO)

Funds received in CELO could be staked via stCELO for additional yield. Lower priority — most donations are in USDT.

## Events

| Event | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `YieldGenerated(address indexed clusterWallet, uint256 yieldUsdt, uint256 yieldSlearn)` | Yield earned from idle funds |
| `YieldDistributed(address indexed clusterWallet, uint256 clusterShare, uint256 pdjShare)` | Yield split between cluster and pdJ |
