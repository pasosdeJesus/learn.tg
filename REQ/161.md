### 2. Course Rewards

| Reward | Amount (configured) | Formula (per user) | Notes |
|--------|---------------------|--------------------|-------|
| **USDT** | 1 USDT per guide | `floor(configuredUsdt × profileScore / 100)` | USDT uses 6 decimals internally (1 USDT = 1,000,000); paid from course vault; subject to availability |
| **SLEARN** | 5 SLEARN per guide | `floor(configuredSlearn × profileScore / 100)` | SLEARN uses 2 decimals internally (1 SLEARN = 100); paid from course vault; subject to availability |

**Formula detail:** Both amounts are in smallest units. `floor()` integer division. With `profileScore = 50` → 0.5 USDT + 2.5 SLEARN. With `profileScore = 100` → 1 USDT + 5 SLEARN.

**When vault balance is insufficient:** The platform notifies the user that the scholarship is deferred. The next donation to the vault enables the next user who completes a guide to receive the scholarship. This creates a virtuous cycle: users can monitor the vault, donate to activate it, and complete a guide quickly to recover their donation and earn the rest.

**Course price and discounts:**

| Country | Price (USDT) | Price (SLEARN, with 20% discount) |
|---------|--------------|-----------------------------------|
| Sierra Leone | $2.00 | 35 SLEARN |
| Colombia | $5.00 | 88 SLEARN |
| United States | $10.00 | 176 SLEARN |
| Other countries | Based on HDI | Based on HDI |

**Scholarship split:** When a student earns a scholarship in this course, part goes to the student's wallet and part goes to the cluster fund (or country fund if the student's church is not in a cluster). See `LearnTGVaultsV5` for implementation details.

### 3. Guide 1: What is Global Disciples?

**Learning objective:** Understand the mission and vision of Global Disciples and why this course matters.

**Content outline:**
- What is Global Disciples? (history, mission, global reach)
- The vision for a country where Global Disciples doesn't have presence: why church clusters matter
- The vision for a country where Global Disciples already has presence
- How the pdJ ecosystem supports the mission (learn.tg + SLEARN + SBTs)
- How the referral program works (activated upon payment)
- Why this course is premium: your payment supports the cluster system

**Reward:** 1 USDT + 5 SLEARN (via formula above, subject to vault availability)

**Crossword:** 3-5 questions testing understanding of GD mission, pdJ ecosystem role, and referral program basics.

**Action:** After this guide, the pastor/leader/member should understand the GD model and the course structure.

**Glossary (to be included at the start of the guide):**

| Term | Simple Definition |
|------|-------------------|
| **SLEARN** | A digital token earned by learning. Can be used to pay for courses or support others. |
| **USDT** | A stable digital currency (1 USDT = 1 USD). |
| **Cluster / network of churches** | A group of 3 or more churches working together for a common purpose. |
| **SBT (Soulbound Token)** | A digital certificate that proves you have completed a course or achieved something. It cannot be transferred to another person. |
| **Cluster fund** | A shared fund (in digital currencies) that the cluster uses for its expenses (travel, materials, etc.). |
| **Ranking** | A public list showing which clusters are most active and transparent. It does not measure the "spiritual value" of churches. |

**Note on pdJ:** "pdJ" refers to "Pasos de Jesús", the organization that facilitates this course and the learn.tg platform. pdJ acts as a bridge between churches and Global Disciples, but does not replace GD's formal process.

## Accessibility: Reaching Pastors Without Smartphones or Literacy

Sierra Leone has pastors and church leaders who cannot read well, do not own a smartphone, or both. The platform must not exclude them. These are **Phase 2+** ideas for making the course accessible beyond the digital-native audience.

### In-Person + Digital Hybrid

| Scenario | Solution |
|----------|----------|
| Pastor with smartphone takes course | Standard flow — self-service on learn.tg |
| Pastor without smartphone but in a group | **Facilitator mode:** one smartphone shared among 3-5 pastors. Facilitator reads guides aloud, group discusses, facilitator marks crossword answers. One wallet per group or per pastor (wallet can be on facilitator's device). |
| Pastor without smartphone, alone | **Printed materials:** PDF/printable version of each guide (~1 page, 400-600 words). Pastor studies offline, then visits a facilitator or cybercafé to complete the crossword and claim rewards. |
| Pastor who cannot read | **Audio guides:** each guide recorded as 3-5 minute audio (English, Krio, Mende, Temne). Listen on shared smartphone, radio, or MP3 player. |

### Technical Enablers (Phase 2+)

| Feature | Description | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Audio recordings** | MP3 per guide, downloadable, playable offline. Hosted on learn.tg alongside text. | High |
| **Printable PDF** | Each guide as a single-page PDF with the crossword at the bottom. Generatable from the markdown source. | High |
| **Group/facilitator mode** | One authenticated user can mark progress for multiple pastors (name + wallet per pastor). SBTs and rewards go to each pastor's wallet. | Medium |
| **Offline crossword** | Complete crossword offline, sync when back online. Already partially supported by the existing crossword system. | Medium |
| **WhatsApp delivery** | Guide text + crossword questions delivered via WhatsApp bot. Pastor responds with answers, bot records progress. | Low |
| **Radio/podcast distribution** | Audio guides distributed via community radio stations or SD cards for MP3 players. | Low |

### Design Principle

> The platform serves those with technology, but the mission serves all. Technology is the tool, not the gatekeeper. Where technology fails to reach, the body of Christ — facilitators, printed pages, audio, radio — carries the Word.

*"I became all things to all people, that by all means I might save some."* (1 Corinthians 9:22)
